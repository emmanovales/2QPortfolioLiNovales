All About Cats: Popular Cat Breeds

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They are known for their friendly and intelligent nature.

Size: 10 - 16 inches in height, larger compared to other domestic cats.

Weight: 4.8 - 8.2kg

Color: Various colors and patterns, including tabby, solid, and bi-color.

Lifespan: 10-13 years on average, with the oldest recorded age being 31 years.

Personality: Friendly, intelligent, and sociable. They are known for their playful and affectionate nature.

Ragdoll

The Ragdoll is a large and affectionate cat breed known for its striking blue eyes and semi-longhair coat.

Size: 9 - 11 inches in height, with a long and sturdy body.

Weight: 4.5 - 9 kg

Color: Various colors and patterns, including colorpoint, mitted, and bicolor.

Lifespan: 12-17 years on average, with the oldest recorded age being 38 years.

Personality: Friendly, affectionate, and sociable. They are known for their gentle and laid-back nature.
